How it works

Two minutes to open. A lifetime to compound.

Amplifi Spark is a cashback and family gifting platform built to help the whole family contribute to a child's Junior ISA.

01
Open in two minutes
Create your child's Spark account from your phone. Link your child's Junior ISA. Start earning cashback from everyday shopping. Done.
02
Invite the whole family
Share your child's gifting page with grandparents, aunts, uncles — anyone who would otherwise give cash for birthdays and Christmas. Every gift goes straight into the ISA and compounds.
03
Watch it grow
The family dashboard shows every contributor, every pound contributed, and exactly what the pot is projected to reach. The earlier you start, the more dramatic the line.
